Project

■ Investigate of Group Dynamics in VR Group Discussions:

In recent years, social VR has become increasingly important, and VR group discussions have attracted a lot of attention. Indeed, VR group discussion is used in business, education, etc. This project is to understand the mechanisms of group dynamics that occur in VR group discussion. For the survey, both objective measurement (e.g., avatar's behavior in VR space, user's behavior in real space) and subjective evaluation (e.g., questionnaire) are used.
